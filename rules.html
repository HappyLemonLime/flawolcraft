How do I know if something is allowed or not on the server?

The minecraft server follows most of the same rules located in the ⁠rules and info channel on the discord but along with that please refrain from:

(Click the rules with a ▶ for more info)

1. Don't Discriminate Against Others.

Don't be racist, transphobic or any of the sort. Doing so will result in an imediat ban


2. Respect People's Identities.

3. Do NOT Grief Unprotected Builds.

Even if a build has not been claimed that does not mean that you can break / steal / kill anything that owned by someone.


4. Do not create any NSFW content on the server.

No NSFW Content, such as any builds or map art that may convey any NSFW ideas and or imagery


5. Have common sense!

6. No cheating / hacking. If you are not sure, don't use it or ask a mod!

No use of hacked / cheating clients. Doing so will resualt in a ban from the server. This includes things like x-ray packs.


What mods are allowed on the SMP?

1. Anything that improves the performance of your game, such as OptiFine / Sodium Click for more info

Allowed mods in this section are OptiFine,Sodium,Lithium,Sodium Extra,Dynamic FPS and any other mod that simply provides client side optimizations.

  • Clients such as Lunar Client and all of the mods found in there, please note this does not include " hacked / cheating clients "
  • anything that is just visual (besides x-ray), such as texture / resource packs and Shaders.

What mods are slightly allowed on the SMP?

Anything where the main functionality is allowed but some parts fall under the banned mods section. Click for more info

Allowed mods in this section are #1 Litematica Both the built in "Easy Place" and the addon for Litematica "Litematica Printer" are not allowed. And #2 ReplayMod. With ReplayMod I just ask you not to use it to see underground and try and look for things. E.g: Looking underground for structures such as Dungeons,strongholds,Buried Treasure,Trial Chambers or any other structures. These are mods where their main functionality can be used, however some parts of the mod can't be used and when caught may result in ban or other punishment from the FlawolCraft server.
